THE SETAI TEL AVIV

The Setai Tel Aviv offers the ultimate luxurious vacation, the perfect opportunity to walk in the footsteps of history and discover Old Jaffa. Every wall and stone symbolize the rich heritage of this luxury getaway, founded by the Ottoman Empire in the 19th century on the remnants of a 10th century Crusader fortress and considered a significant landmark of yesteryears. Located on the famous Clock Square, the city’s most sought-after location, The Setai Tel Aviv stands majestically on the promenade, overlooking endless views of the Mediterranean. A short meander will lead you to the nearby flea market, points of interest and the opportunity to explore narrow ancient streets and alleys, decorated with picturesque houses.
