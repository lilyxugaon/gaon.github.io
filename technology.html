ParaZero Limited

Founded in 2014, ParaZero is dedicated to unlocking the maximum potential of the global drone industry. ParaZero provides smart, intuitive solutions for commercial and professional consumers in the drone market, advancing the drone industry by designing, developing, and delivering high-quality autonomous safety systems.

Arbe Robotics

Arbe (NASDAQ: ARBE), a global leader in next-generation 4D imaging radar chipset solutions, is leading the radar revolution to enable today's truly safe driver assistance systems while paving the way for fully autonomous driving. Arbe's imaging radar is 100 times more detailed than any other radar on the market and is a must-have sensor for L2+ and greater autonomy. The company is providing advanced sensing and paradigm-changing sensing capabilities for automakers, Tier 1 suppliers, autonomous ground vehicles, commercial and industrial vehicles, and a wide range of security applications.

MobileODT

MobileODT is an Israeli FemTech company, founded with a vision to revolutionize the cervical cancer screening and treatment domain. MobileODT offers complete turnkey cervical cancer screening and treatment solutions offering practitioners the convenience of addressing all their screening and treatment needs with a single provider.

SimilarWeb

SimilarWeb provides a free online platform for website rankings and competitive data analysis. The online platform works on big data analytics techniques including web/mobile app traffic and user engagement. Users can simply enter a domain name to assess the popularity of the website and monitor the performance of the website in real time. In addition, we can grasp the dynamics of industry competitors in order to increase users' own network traffic and market share. Similaeweb was officially listed on the New York Stock Exchange in May 2021.

InfinityAR USA INC.

InfinityAR's technology turns any device into a powerful content enhancement platform using basic, inexpensive hardware – a simple stereo camera. Its advanced augmented reality software engine enables 3D digital scenes to accurately represent an individual's current physical environment. This allows users to intuitively interact with enhanced content in the physical environment through natural hand movements.
